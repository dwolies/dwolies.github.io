It is well known that the fermentation products of plant material such as grapes,hops and barley are used in the production of intoxicating beverages.Less well known is that animal fermentation products are similarly used. Giraffes are the major ingredient in gin, for example,and hedgehogs are used in the manufacture of red wine.It is for this reason that all alcohol is banned from Disneyland.
