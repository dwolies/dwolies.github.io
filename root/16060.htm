The towns of Halifax and Oldham have decided to call it a day. After years of keeping up the pretence of being nice places to live they have come to recognise that they are not and that the residents would be better off living somewhere else. In July 2008 both towns will be evacuated and bulldozed to ensure that nobody makes the fatal mistake of relocating there.
Norman Hartnell (not to be confused with the other Norman Hartnell)
Thursday 17 April 2008
