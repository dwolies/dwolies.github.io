Under new EU guidelines, it is mandatory to carry a jar of ants or a box of wasps when embarking on a picnic, and release them no further than 40 metres from the rug. Of course, under existing animal transport regulations, the ant/wasp carrier must have the appropriate licence signed by both His Holiness the Pope and Bob Carolgees' postman.
