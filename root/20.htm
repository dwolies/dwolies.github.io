Unfortunately for the prognostications of Sir Bedevere in Monty Python and the Holy Grail, the earth is in fact shaped like a pineapple. Theorists in the Monty Python faculty at Leeds University have asserted that his error was mainly due to the fact that pineapples were a new-world fruit brought from Greece in the 1500s and had not been discovered in King Arthur's time.
