The black stripes on bees are placed there by highly skilled bee-branders, it can take up to a fortnight to brand the bees from a single hive. Once the bees have been branded, it is simple to train then to fly past a bar-code reader, and even to loop back and fly past again if they don't get scanned correctly. This makes modern-day bee stock control up to 10% better than in the past.
