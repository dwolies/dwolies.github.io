By an amazing coincidence, the song "Brimful Of Asha" was written at exactly the same time by five different bands: Kula Shaker, Iron Maiden, Cornershop, Wet Wet Wet, and a student band from Aberdeen called Stirring Cabbage. Cornershop won the right to record the song in a game of pontoon to which Stirring Cabbage were - rather unfairly - not invited.
