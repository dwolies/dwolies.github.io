The brontosaurus, far from being a slow-witted vegetarian, was possessed of a highly advanced culture, and its favourite food was the flesh of unwary time travellers. The modern view is due largely to fake evidence planted by the brontosauri themselves to ensure that tasty visitors from the future, unaware of the creatures' true nature, would unwittingly place themselves in a position to be caught and eaten.
