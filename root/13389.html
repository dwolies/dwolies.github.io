Damian Hirst is to retire from the world of art and become his final exhibit. He has devised a machine that will, on the touch of a button, slice him in half from head to toe and drop him in a tank of formaldehyde. Art critics are delighted by this bold statement and consider his last work to be vital for the future credibility of the British art scene.
