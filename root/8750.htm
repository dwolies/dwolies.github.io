Despite advertising "dirty deeds done dirt cheap", a recent survey placed Australian rock band AC/DC 4th most expensive in a list of dirty deed suppliers, although they did drop to 6th place when prices were adjusted to take into account the dirtiness of the deeds on offer. The cheapest dirty deeds turned out to be available from Canadian-fronted American rockers Skid Row, with Twisted Sister a close second.
