It is a well known fact that in the chariot race of 'Ben Hur' a small car can be seen in the background. However, people tend to miss the fact that Charlton Heston's character was quite intelligent and may have 'invented' that small car in order to make good his escape. Unfortunately we will never know as in the closing scenes he was electrocuted by a falling pylon.
