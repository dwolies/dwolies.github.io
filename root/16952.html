Argos publish a secret underworld catalogue which allows subscribing villans to purchase weapons, slaves, drugs and maps of the sewers at any of their outlets nationwide. This was only discovered when an elderly woman was recently upset to find that due to an error in completing the order code she had purchased 10 kilos of weapons grade plutonium instead of the dangly harlequin necklace she'd intended to give her daughter as a wedding present.
