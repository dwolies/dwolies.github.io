Fans of the Doors, the seminal psychedelic rock band that came out of Los Angeles in the 1960s, have speculated for years about whether Jim Morrison, the band's lead singer and shamanic poet, faked his own death in Paris in 1969. The truth is that Morrison did not fake his death, but he did fake his own birth with the help of Doors keyboardist Ray Manzarek.
