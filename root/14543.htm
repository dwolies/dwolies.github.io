Jiminy Cricket wasn't a cricket at all.. The plausible theory has its roots in the American occupation of the Philippines at the turn of the 20th century. U.S. soldiers, afflicted with lice, referred to these insects by their Tagalog term, "Jim-Cricks". Eventually, these soldiers returned to the U.S. mainland, carrying their own version of the word with them.
