The lie of today, Sunday the 10th of August 2025
 
Prior to WWII, the UK's favorite musical lyric was "With a hey nonny nonny" scribed by none other the Elizabethan folk legend William Shakespeare. Now of course, ABC's classic "I hope and I pray that maybe someday you'll walk in the room with my heart" is our most celebrated lyrical acheivement.
