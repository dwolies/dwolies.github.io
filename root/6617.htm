The Pilsbury Dough Boy is a famous DJ in Germany, under the name DJ PDB. He was ranked #3 DJ in the world by Muzik Magazine and #5 in the world by Spin Magazine. On stage, he performs a fascinating live show where he morphs his body into different shapes, all coinciding with his big beat sound and accompanied by a brilliant laser show.
Brian Scholer
Sunday, 27 April 2003
