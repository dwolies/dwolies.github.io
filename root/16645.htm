Mankind's dominance of the planet Earth is under threat from an unexpected source. For centuries it has been believed that man acheived dominance due to the evolutionary process that gave us the opposable thumb which enabled us to use tools with more dexterity that other animals. Deep in the Amazon jungle, a tree frog has been discovered that has both opposable thumbs and big toes, giving it twice the manual dexterity of humans. If left unchecked, the evolutionary processes that gave us our position on the planet will elevate the descendants of this tree frog into the dominant species, making us their slaves. We must act swiftly and cut and burn what is left of the Amazonian rainforests to wipe out this slimy green and red threat. Then we must wipe out the pandas (shifty eyes) and finally complete the wholesale slaughter of the dolphins....anything that smiles all the time must be up to something.
