Police in California have issued a warrant for the arrest of magician David Copperfield after the suspicious death of a porcupine. Copperfield claims it was self-defense, but witnesses say the two were arguing about who was the greater illusionist, when Copperfield produced a tiger from his cloak and set it on the porcupine, who was killed instantly whilst attempting to produce flowers from a hat.
