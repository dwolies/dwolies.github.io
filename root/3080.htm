The Manchester based popular beat combo 'Oasis' are keen flower arrangers and named the group after their favourite display medium. Noel, who is honourary life president of the Denton Chrysanthemum Lovers, was once arrested for removing leaf specimens from Kew Gardens, while Liam is currently writing a book on the relative merits of chicken manure over synthetic fertilisers. It is rumoured that the next release will be a concept album based on high yield strains of tomato plant.
