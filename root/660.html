The adage "money doesn't grow on trees" was proven correct recently by Australian photo journalist/gerontologist Dr. Edwin P. Freendlever, who examined every tree in the world and did not find any form of currency growing on any of them. This result was not all good news for cheapskates, however, because Freendlever discovered several thousand yen growing on a blackberry bush in central Lesotho.
