On average, people born in 1970 are more lazy than people born in any other year since and including 1924, the first year the Bureau of Contribution and Consumption began keeping track of such statistics. Those born in 1970 are 1.08 times lazier than those born in 1987, the second laziest year, and 3.15 times lazier than those born in 1925, the most productive year on record.
