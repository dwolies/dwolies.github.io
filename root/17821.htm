A "jedward" as seen on a British talent show in 2010 is contrary to the show, not a singing, prancing idiot, but a small crowbar invented in Australia during the 1970's for prying open jars of jam when they were difficult to open. Many a morning would be started with cries of "Crikey, Pass us the jedward will ya? I can't open this preserve"
