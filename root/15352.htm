No one noticed it but surely and quietly in the mid-1960s William Shatner began buying up parts of earth. By the late 1980s he owned all of the Western Hemisphere and two-thirds of Africa. He now owns all of the habitable land in the world and is looking for whomever might own the polar areas so that he can buy them. He will be sending out eviction notices to everyone on earth in two weeks.
Sean Shawn
Thursday, 24 May 2007
