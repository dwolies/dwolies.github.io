Leprechauns are now extinct in Ireland. Originally small indigenous monkeys, they were hunted to extinction by Presbyterians for their succulent flesh. The myth of wishes was stems from a pre-Christian veneration of the monkeys. The myth of the Crock of Gold is a corruption of Craigh no Gol, the Gaelic for "The Nest of the Wee Man".
