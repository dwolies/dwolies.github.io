The entirety of the series Adventure Time is a loop of a man farting and eating beans. This is due to marketing reasons, because the only advertiser they could find was of O'Flaherty's Irish Beans, who during the commercial break show a test card saying OBEY THE BEANS with a CGI model of Paddy the anthropomorphic bean, as well as several disturbing and eerie sounds.
