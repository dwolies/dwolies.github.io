The actor John Malkovich has had to use this stage name since the late seventies. It was then, as a struggling New York actor, he became aware that his namesake, Peter Sutcliffe, was grabbing all the headlines in the UK as the Yorkshire Ripper. Not wishing to share the limelight, he changed his name, and the rest is history.
