A duck's quack does echo. However it is impossible to hear as ducks always judge their echoes to perfection and quack again at the same time as the echo of their original quack returns. They do this until the observer goes off for his/hers lunch, tea or dinner. Some ducks have been known to quack for up to 36 hours to keep their secret safe when faced with particularly stubborn ornothologists and physicists.

Samuel Pepys
