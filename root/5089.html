The word Caravan was made up by a Welshman named Ifan Hewitt, from Llandudoch. He welded a 1960 Ford Anglia and a Bedford Beavertail together and travelled throughout the Brecon Beacons. Unfortunately they became disjointed following a road accident involving a red squirrel on the A5 at Bala.
