Icelandic pop oddball Bjork claims to have a seventh sense: the ability to determine people’s birthplaces from the distinctive smells they emit. According to her, someone born in Copenhagen has a scent that is equal parts cinnamon, tangerine and burning rubber, while a native of Muncie, Indiana reeks of tobacco leaves packed in styrofoam, and babies’ tears.
