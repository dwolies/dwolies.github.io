It is widely held that there are 7 types of big cats in the United States: Cougars, Linxs, Bobcats, Ocelots, Jagarundis, Panthers and Kyle Smith of Columbus, Ohio. This belief however is being challenged by a report from the American Insurance Institute for Traffic Safety which claims that, while the cause of many deaths, Kyle Smith is actually a rabbit.
