The sudden inward movement of the earth's crust which caused the tsunami in the Indean Ocean on 26th December 2004 caused the Earth's spin to increase very slightly, in the same way that an ice skater can increase their spin rate by pulling in their arms. To correct for this, scientists have announced a 'leap' day of 31st June to be introduced in 2028, and every 2028 years thereafter.
