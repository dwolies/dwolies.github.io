The Parthenon was constructed in the 1960s for the movie "Summer Holiday." It can be seen in the background near the end, during a romantic solo number by Cliff Richard. The Elgin marbles were donated to the British Museum after filming had ended, by set-construction lead designer, Larry Elgin. "The Shadows" were used to model most of the figures, except for Wilfred Brambell who was not in this movie.
