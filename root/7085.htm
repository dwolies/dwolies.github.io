The Cardinals in Rome have been put on alert after a priest noticed that the Pope had put his mitre up for auction on E-bay. After a search through the vaults of the Vatican, several important artifacts such as the only surviving copy of the Necronomicon, the bones of St Paul and a nail from the true cross had already been sold by the pontiff.
