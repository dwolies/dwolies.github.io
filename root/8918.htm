Margaret Thatcher is the 1867 creation of a 19th century engineer, Ernesto Vasquez. Mrs. Thatcher's dynamism can be attributed to a coal fired steam engine and whose graceful limb movement is controlled through the manipulation of a complex, yet elegant, system of pulleys. Margaret is considered the only success of the Thatcher series, which includes the Mark model - a resounding failure.
