Before the industry-standard Ingres and Postgres databases were constructed, the 'Pregres' database was widely used. Its chief feature was being able to deliver the data you requested before you actually requested it. However, when people found that this 'data' was usually guesswork on the part of the server, the slower but more accurate Ingres and Postgres versions became more popular.
