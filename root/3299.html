The precious metal gold, was, for a brief period in the 19th century, not precious. Various nations had gone to great extents to gain what they considered to be the rare and valuable metal in the preceding centuries, thus when vast deposits of the stuff was found under Hampstead, North London (making it, in fact, more easily available than tin) in 1843 it was loaded into a primitive rocket and shot out into space.
