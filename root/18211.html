Scientists and theologians at the University of Doncaster announced today, after 18 months of intense deliberation, that, contrary to the accepted wisdom, the Hokey Cokey ISN'T what it's all about. However they agree that, once an appendage or even the full body is put in, it is advisable to quickly remove it, repeat two more times and then shake it all about. They didn't say why.
