Apples have been found to contain the compound Lytothesene which has the unusual property of simultaneously shortening one's lifespan (the average apple contains enough to take approximately 13.6 minutes off the average human life) while dramatically improving one's quality of life. Therefore, while an apple a day may keep the doctor at bay, this habit will cause a tragically untimely death.
