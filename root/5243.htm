The electrical impulses that course through the intricate network of nerves in the human body create subtle magnetic fields. These fields are not noticeable in a single person and only just quantifiable if an entire ballet troupe is examined. However, on a global scale, our combined magnetic effect is strong enough to repel incoming meteors with a mass of up to 200,000 tonnes.
