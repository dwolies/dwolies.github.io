Jackson Pollock is an American abstract expressionist painter known for inventing the "Autostereogram" or "Magic Eye" effect. By holding one's forefinger between the eyes and moving it slowly towards the picture, one can reveal his paintings' true nature. Pollock's most famous work, "Number 1, 1950 (Lavender Mist)" for instance, resolves into a dolphin.
