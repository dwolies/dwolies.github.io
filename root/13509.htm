In 1846 William Chortlesworth penned the children’s song ‘Row, row, row the boat’. However the version we know today was not the original. In 1972 archivists discovered Chortlesworth’s original manuscripts and put them up for auction. They were bought by the then lead singer of Genesis, Peter Gabriel, and he later adapted the tune to become his major hit ‘Sledgehammer’.
