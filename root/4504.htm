In addition to the well known Bermuda Triangle, there are in fact a number of other geometric shapes on the surface of the earth that are subject to other-worldly influences. These include the Uppsala Rhomboid, the Batavian Tangram, the Hudson Bay Semicircle, the Tierra Del Fuego Octahedron, and the Great Pyramid of Dandenong.
