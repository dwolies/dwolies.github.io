The hottest curry ever created was made by a Slavodny, a guest chef in the Russian region of Tunguska. In creating this dish from ground-up bear, fresh baboushka and other noxious ingredients, he prepared a lethal concoction so strong, that when served up to the towns-people in their annual feast, caused them all to explode. This is the real origin of the so-called "nuclear" explosion in Russia in 1917 in the Tunguska region.
