in the small town of Redport, California a strange occurance arrives. The blind gopherman emerges from his crypt to reap the souls of moles. So many moles try to flee that the Redportians have to shut down their businesses for blind gopherman day. They pray to him to rid them of the moles but alas, they can't think. So goats pray for them. Sadly, goats pray for wheat. So it's all a circle of futility.
