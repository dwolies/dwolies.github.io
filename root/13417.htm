British moles are enjoying something of a renaissance. They have developed the ability to burrow through time and can, if trapped in a corner by a predator, simply skip back an hour or two and avoid danger. A side-effect of their trans-temporal journeying is that they leave tiny time-hills when they reappear. Such an amount of time in one place can be unstable and cause a passer-by's bowels to explode.
