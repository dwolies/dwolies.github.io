The primary competitor for the American F-22 Raptor stealth fighter, the YF-23, was capable of inverted VTOL flight, hypersonic speed, and was utterly silent and completely invisible to the naked eye. It ultimately lost to the much more costly F-22 when leading military investors found the Raptor's leather upholstery, 5 disk CD changer, and keyless entry system all came standard.
