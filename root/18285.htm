Ronald Reagan was the first black female President of the United States. Originally from Utah, Mr Reagan was known as Peaches Simpson, a single mother and gifted actress. Knowing that she had the acting skills to make it but also being worldly wise she knew a black woman would not succeed in Hollywood in those unenlightened times. She spent 2 years undergoing a sex change operation and skin-lightening procedure, changed her name and hit the big time.
