Hemel Hempstead is hatching a plan to rid its streets of the large number of undead who are becoming a nuisance to shoppers. Together with Luton Town Council, they will offer a lucrative contract of work involved in a secretive town regeneration scheme but once on site will be bombarded with silver bullets, holy water, rue, garlic and photos of Celine Dion.
