In order to compete with Sega's new Dreamcast games console, Nintendo are planning the launch of a new game for the N64, the likes of which have never before been seen in the gaming world. The game, provisionally titled "Authex", is loosely based on the popular word-processing format, its object being to type a complete novel, either against the clock or against an opponent.
