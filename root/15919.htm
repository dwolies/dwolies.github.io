When a shoe shine sponge (available from most supermarkets and shoe suppliers) is rubbed gently onto the eyeball, it negates the requirement - and indeed the ability - to blink for up to 2 days. This is especially useful for anyone trying to remain awake for a specific purpose and is also handy for unnerving small children whilst on public transport.
