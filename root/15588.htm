Sky Diving was originally a form of Dutch torture. But in 1836 a scientist discovered that the victims (with parachutes) had lived. This led to the discovery of microwave popcorn, and the fact that every victim of the sky dive torture lived and changed their name to Beverly Hill. That is also the origin of Beverly Hills, since thats where the tortured lived afterward.
