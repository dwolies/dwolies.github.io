The lies in "Dave's Web of Lies" come from a variety of sources. Forty percent are hand-picked in Italian vineyards. Thirty percent are dredged from Lake Baikal. Twenty-five percent are removed from the knees of knee surgery patients. The last five percent originate from a man on the island of Madagascar, who presents them annually to the Queen in a formal ceremony.
