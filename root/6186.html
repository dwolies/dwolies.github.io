It is a little known fact that the Polyester was a rare mammal found deep in the jungles of Thailand. It's primary colors were brown, tan, yellow, lime green, or powder blue. It is also a close relative of the Argyle, which was found in the Falklands and a distant cousin to the Madras, found in ancient Persia. In the late 70s, this rare oddity was hunted to extinction for the use of making leisure suits and tacky tuxedos.
