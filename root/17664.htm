The common squint is generally misunderstood as a muscular imbalance in the eye, when in actual fact it is merely the eye, due to its inquisitive nature, trying to look at the other eye and can be cured simply by using a mirror to allow the "wandering" eye to see its opposite and put its mind at rest.
