'Dave's Web of Lies' was originally developed as a tool of propaganda during the Falklands War. Pamphlets were dropped on Argentina featuring such fibs as, 'Margaret Thatcher is made of titanium' and 'English soldiers are impervious to bullets' and 'The Falkland Islands were made in Ipswich in 1969, then shipped and moored at their present location six years later'.
