The refusal by the US Government to adopt the metric system as used by the rest of the world is a policy which dates back to the Reagan-era "War against drugs". Unable to conceal the existence of mind-altering substances, the anti-drug task force, has attempted to conceal the existence of the units in which they are measured, namely grammes and kilos, in the hope people will be unable to make purchases.
