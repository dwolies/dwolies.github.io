Christian scientists have recently claimed that they have unearthed the body of Jesus Christ in an archaeological dig in southern California. The body's skeleton was found almost completely intact, with very little decay on the 2000 year old bones. Among the other items recovered at the site were a golden chalice, a shroud of sacred cloth, and a pair of red Converse All-Star Chuck Taylors signed by all twelve apostles.

Brian Scholer
Friday 24 January 2003
