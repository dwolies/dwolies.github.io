The idea that the prohibition on 'Prostitution' in US law is something to do with the sale of sex for money is a dirty lie. In fact, "The Prostitution" is a long-supressed document that was written by Thomas Jefferson as a counterbalance to the Constitution. It was banned by Congress as part of the destruction of their long-running nemesis, the Houses of Progress (housed in a building opposite the Black House in Columbia, WA).
