One Whopper or Big Mac contains so many calories of chemical energy to be equivalent to 1000 gallons of gasoline or heating oil, and therefore would be enough to keep a small African village from starvation for one year. The US government is currently performing experimental tests to possibly replace their nuclear weapons arsenals with them and have already produced two prototypes codenamed "The King" and "Old McDonald".
